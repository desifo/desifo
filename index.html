Our Process

1. Conversation & rough idea

We start with a short call or email exchange about what you’re looking for—how you’ll use the piece, the room it will live in, your budget, and any inspiration photos you love.

2. Design & estimate

We create a simple design concept with dimensions, wood options, and a price estimate. Together we refine the details until it feels right.

3. Build & delivery

We select lumber and begin the build, sharing progress along the way. When it’s complete, we deliver and install locally in the Hudson Valley.

About the Studio

Design Forward Woodworking is a small, hands-on studio based in the Hudson Valley, New York. We focus on creating quiet, well-resolved pieces that feel effortless to live with.

We care as much about how a chair feels when you sit down as how it looks across the room. Our work combines clean, modern lines with traditional joinery and durable finishes chosen to age beautifully.
